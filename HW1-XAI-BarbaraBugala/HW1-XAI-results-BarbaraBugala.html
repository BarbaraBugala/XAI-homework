Task A

$v(\emptyset) = 0 \\ v(A) = 20 \\ v(B) = 20 \\ v(C) = 60 \\ v(A,B) = 60 \\ v(A,C) = 70 \\ v(B,C) = 70 \\ v(A,B,C) = 100 $

$v(A) - v(\emptyset) = 20$
$v(A,B) - v(B) = 40$
$v(A,C) - v(C) = 10$
$v(A,B,C) - v(B,C) = 30$

number of players $ \rightarrow |P| = 3$

shap values for player A:

$\phi_A = \frac{2}{6} *20 + \frac{1}{6}* 40 + \frac{1}{6} *10 + \frac{2}{6}* 30 = 25$
